Conclusion — why it matters

So what? Publishing the actual model files — solution-stripped, re-solvable, with an honest slimming ledger — is the simulation equivalent of publishing code: anyone with COMSOL can open, inspect, and re-run every claim this site makes about voids, thermal gradients, or fixture stress. The library also shows range: electrochemical impedance physics, coupled heat transfer, and contact mechanics, each validated against a published anchor or a second engine.
