Research outputs

Peer-reviewed and in-preparation work behind the methods demonstrated here. Unpublished laboratory results are not reproduced on this public site.

Anode-free LLZO solid-state cells under operando GEIS-AA lead author — manuscript in preparation

UCSB, Sakamoto group. An operando-diagnostics and censored-reliability program on an anode-free solid-state Li-metal platform. Unpublished; described qualitatively here. The public pages on this site demonstrate the same analysis methods on openly licensed datasets.

Operando detection of Li void formation by single-frequency impedance co-first author

ACS Electrochemistry, 2026. A single-frequency impedance signal reaches its detection threshold at lower stripped capacity than cell polarization, on both oxyhalide and garnet cells; mechanism supported by a 3D equivalent-circuit void model (simulation run by collaborators — I prepared the cells and validated predictions against the operando measurements).

Conformal hetero-electrolyte interface between soft oxyhalides and garnet for low-pressure, Li-reservoir-free solid-state batteries full-cell validation · NMC full-cell assembly

RSC Energy & Environmental Science, 2026. A low-impedance conformal oxyhalide|garnet interface; DRT separation of bulk / grain-boundary / interface contributions; anode-free full cell with high coulombic efficiency at low stack pressure.

Laminar Assembly Polymerization of ionic COF thin films for ion-gated FET integration sole author (BS senior thesis)

Cornell, Zhong group. A synthesis DOE converging on optimal precursor concentrations; ~25 nm film thickness by AFM and purity by Raman; analysis of collaborator-collected GIWAXS (CHESS synchrotron) and FTIR — pore size and imine-bond confirmation (data collection not mine); 6-inch wafer-scale MoS₂ transfer.
